#1 winebottle.blend
3D model of wine-bottle potential created to explain Ginzburg-Landau theory for a poster 
"Higgs mode in supercoductivity observed with THz light" (original title: テラヘルツ光で見る超伝導のヒッグスモード) 
displayed in the exposition of students of the faculty of Physics, School of Science, the Univ. of Tokyo in May Festival 2025(5/24, 25). 
The poster is uploaded to: https://event.phys.s.u-tokyo.ac.jp/physlab2025/posters/PhysicsLab_Poster_Higgs_Mode_1%20(6).pdf
The basic structure is generated by a Python script.
